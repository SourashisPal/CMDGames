This is a TicTacToe game. ♨️
It consists of X and O marks which are to be given by the respective players.
Rules :-
1) Player 1 will be asked for his/her choice which may be X and O.
2) For marking different numbers will be shown for different places.
3) The numbers which will be shown have to be entered correctly.
4) In case the number entered does not match any of the numbers given no action will take place and the turn will be changed.
5) At last, it will be declared whether the match is drawn, or won by any player.
 --Enjoy our game--
